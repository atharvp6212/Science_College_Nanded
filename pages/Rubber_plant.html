Rubber Tree

Hi! Welcome to Science College, Nanded. I am Ficus elastica, commonly known as Rubber plant or Rubber tree, a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia. Known for its large, glossy, dark green leaves, the Rubber plant produces milky latex historically used as a natural rubber source. It is a popular ornamental houseplant, valued for its striking foliage and air-purifying qualities. In its natural habitat, it can grow up to 30-40 meters. The tree produces small, inconspicuous flowers and fruits. The Rubber plant is cherished for its aesthetic appeal and historical significance in natural rubber production.
