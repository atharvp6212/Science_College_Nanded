Mango

Hi! Welcome to Science College, Nanded. I am Mangifera indica, commonly known as the Mango tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to South Asia but cultivated in many tropical and subtropical regions. Renowned for its delicious and sweet fruit, the mango, this tree is one of the most widely cultivated fruit trees in the world. The mango fruit comes in various cultivars, each with its unique flavor, shape, and color. Beyond its culinary appeal, the Mango tree holds cultural significance in many societies and is often associated with festivals and traditional ceremonies. The tree's wood is used in various applications, and different parts of the plant have applications in traditional medicine. The Mango tree's adaptability, economic importance, and flavorful fruits make it a beloved and iconic tree worldwide.
