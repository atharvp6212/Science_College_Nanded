Hi! Welcome to Science College, Nanded. I am Morinda citrifolia, commonly known as Noni, is a small evergreen tree or shrub native to Southeast Asia and Australasia. Recognized for its medicinal properties, various parts of the Noni plant are used in traditional medicine, with the fruit consumed as juice or in supplements for its potential health benefits. Noni has a distinctive knobby fruit and a strong odor. Popular in traditional Polynesian and Indian cultures, Noni is valued for its anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and immune-boosting properties.
