Hi! Welcome to Science College, Nanded. I am Spathodea campanulata, commonly known as the African Tulip Tree or Flame of the Forest, is a large, fast-growing evergreen tree native to tropical Africa. Renowned for its striking, bell-shaped, orange to red flowers, the African Tulip Tree is often planted as an ornamental tree in tropical and subtropical regions around the world. The distinctive flowers, resembling tulips, grow in clusters and create a vibrant display. The tree has large, compound leaves, and its wood is not commonly used commercially due to its softness. Besides its ornamental value, the African Tulip Tree has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes. However, it's important to note that the tree is considered invasive in some areas due to its rapid growth and potential impact on native ecosystems.
