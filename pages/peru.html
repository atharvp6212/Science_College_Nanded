Guava

Hi! Welcome to Science College, Nanded. I am Psidium guajava, or Guava, is a small, evergreen tree celebrated for its distinctive fruit. With round or pear-shaped bodies, guavas boast green or yellow skin and pink or white flesh, packing a nutritional punch with vitamins A and C, fiber, and antioxidants. Widely enjoyed fresh, guavas are also utilized in juices, jams, desserts, and salads, showcasing their versatility in the culinary world. Native to tropical regions, this fruit-bearing tree has become a beloved and nutritious addition to diets globally, appreciated for both its taste and health benefits.
