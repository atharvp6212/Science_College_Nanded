Plumeria pudica

Hi! Welcome to Science College, Nanded. I am Plumeria pudica, commonly known as Bridal Bouquet or White Frangipani, a tropical evergreen shrub native to Central America. The plant is admired for its distinctive spoon-shaped leaves and clusters of beautiful, fragrant white flowers with a yellow center, which bloom throughout the year. It is widely grown as an ornamental plant in gardens and landscapes for its striking appearance and delightful scent. Plumeria pudica is drought-tolerant and requires minimal maintenance, making it a popular choice in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant is cherished for its aesthetic appeal and its ability to attract pollinators like butterflies.
