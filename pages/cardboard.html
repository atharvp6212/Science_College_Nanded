पुठ्ठा पाम

Hi! Welcome to Science College, Nanded. I am Zamia furfuracea, commonly known as the Cardboard Palm, is a cycad native to southeastern Mexico. The Cardboard Palm is recognized for its unique, leathery, and stiff fronds that resemble the texture of cardboard, giving it its common name. It is a popular ornamental plant in gardens and landscapes due to its distinctive appearance and low maintenance requirements. Despite its name, it is not a true palm but rather a cycad. Zamia furfuracea is well-suited for tropical and subtropical climates, and it is often used as a decorative potted plant indoors. As with many cycads, it's important to note that all parts of the Cardboard Palm are toxic if ingested.
