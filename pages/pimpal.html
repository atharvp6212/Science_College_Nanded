Hi! Welcome to Science College, Nanded. I am Ficus religiosa, the Sacred Fig or Peepal tree, is culturally and ecologically significant. Native to the Indian subcontinent, it's a large deciduous tree linked to Hinduism, Buddhism, and Jainism. Planted near temples, it's associated with myths, including the Buddha's enlightenment. With heart-shaped leaves, it's valued in traditional medicine. The tree provides shade, habitat for wildlife, and aids soil conservation. Notably, the Peepal tree releases oxygen day and night, a unique trait. Its longevity and cultural importance make it an iconic species in various traditions.
