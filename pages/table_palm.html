Hi! Welcome to Science College, Nanded. I am Livistona rotundifolia, commonly known as the Footstool Palm or Fan Palm, is an attractive palm species native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia. The Fan Palm is characterized by its large, rounded leaves that form a circular fan-like shape. Its gracefully arching fronds give it an elegant appearance. Livistona rotundifolia is often chosen for landscaping and indoor decoration due to its ornamental value. It is relatively adaptable to various environmental conditions and is appreciated for its low maintenance requirements. With its aesthetic appeal and versatility, the Fan Palm remains a popular choice for those seeking a touch of tropical beauty in their surroundings.
