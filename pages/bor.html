Hi! Welcome to Science College, Nanded. I am Ziziphus mauritiana Lamk, commonly referred to as Indian Jujube or Ber, is a deciduous tree native to South Asia and widely cultivated in tropical and subtropical regions. The tree bears small, round to oval fruits, known as Ber, that range in color from yellow to red or brown, offering a sweet taste. These fruits are not only consumed fresh but also used in the preparation of jams, juices, and traditional sweets. The dried Ber fruits are recognized for their nutritional value and find application in both culinary and medicinal practices. With its versatile uses and adaptability to various climates, Ziziphus mauritiana remains an important and valued tree in many cultures.
